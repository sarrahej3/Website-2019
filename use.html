How I Get my Photos

I use an Iphone xr to get my photos, keep reading if you want to see my step process.

Step one: go on your iPhone home screen

Step two: click on the camera

Step three: slide to portrait mode (with this you can get a clear picture of the main focus in the picture)

Step four: don't take the picture dead on. Turn your camera at an angle so it isnt dead on.

You can choose to edit the picture to enhance it or not, it's your choice really. I like to make the pictures more cool toned rather warm.
